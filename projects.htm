    Just Better Impaling - Just a datapack that makes impaling usable...

    Trident in bedrock are better for a simple reason: impaling work on any mob touching water... This datapack fixes the issue by making the trident as powerfull than an enchanted axe when raining or under water.
    When enchanted with Impaling V, the trident deals 13 damage, but if you deal a crit, this goes up to 17.
